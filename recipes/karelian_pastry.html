Karelian Pastry (Karjalanpiirakka)

Description

Indulge in the delightful flavors of Karelian Pastry, also known as Karjalanpiirakka, a traditional Finnish delicacy hailing from the region of Karelia. These small, thin-rimmed pastries boast a tender rye crust, perfectly enveloping a delightful rice porridge filling. Often enjoyed with a generous spread of butter, the simplicity of Karelian Pastry is its true charm, inviting you to savor the genuine taste of Finland's culinary heritage. Crafted with love and cherished for generations, this pastry is a testament to the rich and authentic flavors that captivate the hearts and palates of all who taste it.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups rye flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1/2 cup short-grain rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the rye flour, all-purpose flour, and salt for the pastry crust. Gradually add the hot water while mixing until the dough comes together. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
  2. Preheat your oven to 475°F (240°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Divide the dough into small, golf-ball-sized portions. On a floured surface, roll out each dough portion into a thin oval shape, about 1/8-inch thick.
  4. In a saucepan, combine the short-grain rice, water, and a pinch of salt for the rice porridge filling. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  5. Stir in the milk and continue cooking over low heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens and resembles a creamy porridge. Stir in the butter and remove from heat. Let the rice porridge filling cool while you prepare the pastry crust.
  6. Spoon a generous amount of the cooled rice porridge filling onto the center of each rolled-out dough oval.
  7. Pinch the edges of the dough to create a thin, raised rim around the rice filling, leaving the center open.
  8. Place the pastries on the prepared baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes or until the pastry crust turns lightly golden brown.
  9. Remove the Karelian Pastries from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

Enjoy your authentic Karelian Pastry with a generous spread of butter, and embrace the true essence of Finnish culinary heritage!
